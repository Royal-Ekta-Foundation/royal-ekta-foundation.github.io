Frequently Asked Questions

What is Royal Ekta Foundation?

Royal Ekta Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting education, healthcare, and sustainable development across India.

How can I support Royal Ekta Foundation?

You can support by donating, volunteering, or partnering with us on various projects. Visit our "Get Involved" page for more details.

Where does Royal Ekta Foundation operate?

We operate mainly in rural and underserved regions of India, focusing on education, healthcare services, and community development programs.

Is my donation to Royal Ekta Foundation tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations to Royal Ekta Foundation are eligible for tax deductions under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, India.

Can I volunteer with Royal Ekta Foundation?

Yes! We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds to help in education, healthcare, event organization, and awareness campaigns. Apply through our Volunteer page.

How are the donations utilized?

Donations are carefully used for running educational programs, healthcare camps, building infrastructure, and supporting local community initiatives.

How can I contact Royal Ekta Foundation?

You can reach us via our Contact Us page, or call our phone number (+91) 9892110014

Does Royal Ekta Foundation provide project updates?

Yes, we regularly update our website and social media pages with progress reports, project stories, and impact reports for transparency.
